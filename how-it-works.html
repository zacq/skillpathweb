Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our coaching process

How much time should I dedicate to coaching each week?

We recommend at least 2-3 hours per week for optimal results. However, our platform is designed to be flexible, allowing you to progress at your own pace. Even 30 minutes of focused practice several times a week can lead to significant improvement over time.

Can I change my development focus areas?

Absolutely! Your development pathway is flexible and can be adjusted at any time. As your goals evolve or as you master certain skills, you can update your focus areas and our AI will recalibrate your personalized plan accordingly.

How does the AI feedback compare to human coaching?

Our AI provides immediate, objective feedback based on best practices and data from thousands of successful professionals. While it doesn't replace the nuanced guidance of human coaches, it offers consistent, available-anytime feedback that complements traditional coaching approaches. Many users find the combination of AI-powered practice with occasional human coaching sessions to be highly effective.
